Hello, I’m still Jesse.

These are my interests.

Yoga

Yoga is one way I connect with my body and spirit. When I practice and teach yoga, I’m practicing mindfulness, which means paying attention to my breath and the way it feels in my body. I tend to practice and teach a slower style of yoga which allows a lot of time to ground the body and notice the breath.

If you’re interested in checking out one of my Facebook Live classes or just chatting more about yoga, feel free to get in touch: clearyjd@gmail.com

Nature

Communing with nature is another way I connect to the world around me. The more time I spend in nature, the more I feel a part of it rather than separate from it.

I enjoy taking long walks in leafy parks, spending time in my garden and observing birds and other wildlife in my own neighborhood.

Cooking & baking

There's nothing quite like the satisfaction of pulling a freshly baked loaf of bread out of the oven. I greatly enjoy drawing inspiration from recipes and putting my own spin on them.

My passion for cooking and baking began in college when I became vegan. Starting from cookbook recipes, I soon discovered my love of innovating and creating new twists on everything from falafel to chickpea salad to hummus.
